About Me

Hello! I am Sreypha Phon a Graphic Design Major at Columbia College In Columbia Missouri and this page is meant to show off my design work and artwork that I have created while being at Columbia College.

I would say that my designs are playful, engaging and illustrative. Before coming to Collage I was a more traditional artist using mainly graphite using my past skill I love including my knowledge of art elements into my Graphic Design work. I may lean toward fun and colorful designs but if a client needs a more modern and professional design I can fit my idea and style into those guidelines as well.
